Privacy Policy – ChainReact

Effective Date: June 7, 2025

ChainReact is built with a strong commitment to your privacy. We believe that habit tracking should be personal, distraction-free, and safe. Here’s how we protect your data:

🔒 No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your progress is stored entirely on your device.

🚫 No Ads or Analytics

ChainReact is 100% free of advertisements, trackers, or third-party analytics. We don’t use cookies or any web beacons.

📴 Offline-First

The app is designed to run fully offline. It does not require internet access for any of its core features.

📂 Your Data Stays With You

All your habit data is stored locally in your browser using modern browser storage. You’re in control — clear your browser storage to reset the app completely.

🤝 Open Source Transparency

The source code is available on GitHub. Feel free to inspect or contribute!
